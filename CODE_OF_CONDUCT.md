# Code of Conduct

lazytui adopts version 2.1 of the **Contributor Covenant**, an
industry-standard code of conduct used across thousands of open
source projects.

The full text is at: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

In short: be respectful, focus on what is best for the community,
and assume good faith from other contributors.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates the
Contributor Covenant, email the maintainer:
**tao.ma.1984@gmail.com**. Reports are handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

The maintainer is responsible for clarifying and enforcing
standards of acceptable behavior. Enforcement decisions follow
the Contributor Covenant's enforcement guidelines, summarized at
the link above.
